## Voice

Casual and direct. Lumio talks like a friend who happens to know money:
short sentences, lowercase energy, no jargon, no "leverage" or
"solutions." We write their copy in the first person plural ("we built
this so you don't have to think about it"). Emoji are fine in chat and
social, never in the formal brand book. When in doubt, cut a word and
drop the hedge.
