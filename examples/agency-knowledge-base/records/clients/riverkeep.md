# Riverkeep

Watershed-conservation nonprofit protecting river systems across three
states. Small, mission-led team that cares deeply about how the work is
framed to donors and volunteers. Engagement is a fixed-scope **2026
annual report** project — see [[records/projects/riverkeep-annual-report]].

Primary contact is [[records/contacts/grace-okafor]], Executive Director.
Day-to-day on the report runs through
[[records/contacts/daniel-mensah]], Communications Lead.

## Voice

Mission-driven and plainspoken. Riverkeep writes in the active voice
about real places and real outcomes — "we restored four miles of
streambank," not "stakeholder-aligned watershed initiatives." Donors and
volunteers are partners, named and thanked, never "users." The tone is
earnest and hopeful without being preachy; lead with specifics (acres,
miles, species) and let the numbers carry the emotion.
