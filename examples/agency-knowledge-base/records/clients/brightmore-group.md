# Brightmore Group

National home-and-lifestyle retailer, 140 stores plus e-commerce. A
mature brand with a thick guidelines document and a real sign-off chain —
nothing public ships without brand and legal review. Engagement is an
ongoing **retainer**; the current flagship work is the holiday campaign,
[[records/projects/brightmore-holiday-campaign]].

Primary contact is [[records/contacts/eleanor-whitfield]], Brand &
Marketing Director and the brand authority. Procurement and contracting
run through [[records/contacts/raymond-cole]], who owns POs and the MSA.

## Voice

Formal and polished. Brightmore speaks in a measured third-person brand
voice: full sentences, Oxford comma, no contractions in headlines,
aspirational but never breathless. Copy must align to the brand
guidelines on file (color, tone, the approved tagline lockups). Assume
every public-facing line will be read by brand and legal before it ships,
so write it to clear review the first time.
