# Approve Halcyon renewal terms

Acme approved the [[records/companies/halcyon]] renewal of the Acme PM
contract at 120 seats on a new two-year term, renewing July 2026 — the
shape Elena asked for in
[[sources/emails/2026-05-22-elena-rodriguez-renewal]]. The commercial
gate to signing is the DPA / security review, not pricing.

[[records/contacts/priya-nair]] confirmed the terms on the security
review [[records/meetings/2026-05-22-halcyon-security-review]]. From the
transcript [[sources/transcripts/2026/05/2026-05-22-halcyon-security-review]]:

> **Priya Nair:** Then let's lock it: 120 seats on a two-year term,
> renewing July, and I'll have Gunderson send a revised DPA this week.
> Decision made on the commercials — the security review is the only open
> item.

Alternatives considered: renew flat at 90 seats year to year (rejected —
Halcyon's ops team had already doubled) or hold for a one-year term
(rejected — Elena asked to lock in a two-year term). Signature is gated
on the DPA; see [[records/decisions/engage-gunderson-halcyon-dpa]].
