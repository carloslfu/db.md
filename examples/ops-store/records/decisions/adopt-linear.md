# Adopt Linear for project tracking

Acme adopted [[records/companies/linear]] on the Standard plan — 40
seats at USD 8 per seat per month, billed monthly — for engineering and
ops issue tracking, replacing an ad-hoc shared spreadsheet.

The decision followed a two-week trial. The terms were confirmed in
writing by [[records/contacts/jordan-mills]] in
[[sources/emails/2026-01-28-jordan-mills-linear-trial]]:

> **Plan:** Standard — unlimited issues, GitHub/Slack sync, the cycle
> and triage views your ops team asked about.
> **Price:** USD 8 per user per month, billed monthly to your Amex.
> **Seats:** 40 to start (covers product + ops).

Alternatives considered: Jira (too heavy for a 40-person org), GitHub
Projects (weaker triage views), and staying on the spreadsheet (the
status quo the move was meant to fix). The recurring cost lands as
[[records/expenses/2026-05-01-linear]] each month and rolls into
[[records/synthesis/vendor-spend]].
