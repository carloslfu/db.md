# Amazon Web Services

Cloud infrastructure provider. Acme runs its product and internal
systems on AWS — EC2, RDS, S3, and CloudFront. It is the single largest
recurring line on the company card.

Billing: usage-based, charged monthly. The May charge is recorded at
[[records/expenses/2026-05-03-aws]]. Spend trend is tracked on
[[records/synthesis/vendor-spend]]; the May invoice came in roughly 8%
above April on the back of higher S3 egress.

No named account manager — billing and support run through the AWS
console, owned internally by [[records/contacts/tom-becker]].
