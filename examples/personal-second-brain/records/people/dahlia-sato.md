call when I'm at a fork in the road. We met around 2019 when she ran the
team I joined.

tells me what to do — on the 05-20 call she reframed the whole IC-versus-
team-lead decision with one question ("which version of the job do you
want to be better at in two years?") instead of an answer. That's her
whole mentoring style, and it's the same way the slip-box works on me.
