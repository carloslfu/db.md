# 2026-05-20

Long call with Dahlia tonight. She was my manager at the agency years
ago and she's still the first person I call when I'm at a fork. Told her
I'm torn between going deeper as an IC and taking the team-lead thing.

question that reframed it: "which version of the job do you want to be
better at in two years?" That's the whole decision, really. I keep
forgetting she answers a question with a better question. Owe her a
proper dinner.

The rest of the weekend went into the home lab. Finally tore down the
ad-hoc nginx config and put Caddy in front of everything, so the
services have real TLS now instead of the self-signed cert I kept
clicking through. Got the Jellyfin instance and the photo backup behind
it. Next is the off-site backup, which I keep putting off because it's
boring and important — the worst combination.

Also: I notice I do my best thinking on these calls with Dahlia the same
way I do it in the slip-box — by being asked a good question, not by
being given an answer. There's a thread there between how she mentors
and what I keep reading about note-taking. Filed for later.
