# Corporate Contributor License Agreement (CCLA)

When contributions come from employees acting on behalf of their
employer, this CCLA scopes those contributions for the corporation.
It does NOT replace the individual ICLA (`CLA.md`).

To sign:

1. An authorized representative of the corporation completes and
   signs this document.
2. Attach the signed PDF to your corporation's first PR (or request a
   contact address from the maintainer), referencing
   `CCLA — db.md — <Corporation>`.
3. Send the list of designated employee logins (`@github-handle`
   form) covered by this CCLA.
